Jax & Pup.

Jax & Pup is a series dedicated to my D&D character (that's not really a D&D charater) Jax and her animal companion Pup. Although, I don't actually play D&D. I designed Jax, but I don't think I'll ever play with her. So, she lives here in my drawings instead.

Jax:

-Tiefling

-21

-Pirate

-Rouge

Pup:

-Shark-Puppy

-Age Unknown

-Animal Companion

-Adorable

Jax is a purple teifling, with shark-like tail. Raised on the seas, she's well versed in the world of pirates. She runs the show and won't let anybody else take control.

she carries a very few things. Most of them basic pirate needs; a compass, a sword, and the keys to the brig. However, She also wears a golden amulet but nobody's actually seen what it looks like.

Essentially, if you haven't learned by know, she's a control freak that nobody knows muhc about.

Her animimal companion, Pup, is the cutest mix between a shark and puppy. He follows her everywhere sticking his nose where it doesn't belong. He may be small but he is fierce. (Pup's art is based off the comic series "Shark Puppy" by VRESS)

Although the chances of me ever playing a game of D&D with Jax are slim, I figured that rolling a character sheet for her would help me to round out who she was as a character.
